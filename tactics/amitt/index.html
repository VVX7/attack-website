AM!TT Tactics

AM!TT Tactics: 12
ID Name Description
TA01 Strategic Planning Defining the desired end state that is the set of required conditions that defines achievement of all objectives.
TA02 Objective Planning Objectives should be clearly defined, measurable, and achievable. Achieving objectives ties execution of tactical tasks to reaching the desired end state. There are four primary considerations:
TA03 Develop People Develop online and offline users and agents, including automated personas
TA04 Develop Networks Develop online and offline communities and transmission methods and cultivate assets and unwitting agents
TA05 Microtargeting Target very specific populations of people
TA06 Develop Content Create and acquire content used in incident
TA07 Channel Selection Set up specific delivery, amplification and manipulation channels for incident, including channels to attack. Each channel serves as an atomic surface (test bed) location/site for adversary to hide/conceal the work/content that goes into setting up for this channel and/or other specific platforms. Contributes to building out a connected ecosystem where this channel can operate as part of a whole campaign/incident
TA08 Pump Priming Release content on a targetted small scale, prior to general release, including releasing seed narratives
TA09 Exposure Release content to general public or push to larger population
TA10 Go Physical Move incident into offline world
TA11 Persistence Keep incident 'alive', beyond the incident creators' efforts
TA12 Measure Effectiveness Measure effectiveness of incident, for use in planning future events
